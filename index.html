Doing Business in Fairfield

A step-by-step guide to help entrepreneurs understand local requirements.

1

Acquire a Business License

All businesses operating within city limits must apply for and maintain an active general business license.

Official Revenue Office
2

Occupational License Fee (ORD 1019)

Depending on your business structure, you may need to submit occupational license fees based on gross receipts or employee count.

Read Ordinance Summary
3

Zoning Check & Approval (ORD 931)

Before signing a commercial lease or operating from home, verify that your business type is permitted in that specific zoning district.

Contact Planning Department
4

Specialty: Food Trucks (ORD 2017-1101)

Mobile food vendors require specific location approvals, health department clearance, and proof of liability insurance.

Insurance proof required prior to permit issuance
5

Fire & Safety Inspection

Commercial spaces and mobile units are subject to pre-opening safety inspections by the Fire Marshal to ensure public safety standards.

Schedule with Fire Department
